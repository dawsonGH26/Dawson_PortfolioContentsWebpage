Background

My name is Dawson Ring. I was born and raised in McIntire, Iowa, until I was six. After that, my mom, my sister, and I moved to Florida, where we lived until I was seventeen, at which point we moved back to McIntire permanently. My experiences in Florida helped me understand and appreciate diversity while still staying firmly grounded in my morals and beliefs. While I was in Florida, I had no clear goal for my career path. It wasn't until we returned to my roots that I discovered my true passion: programming.

When I returned to Iowa and began attending high school, my career path was, to say the least, undefined. Then, one day, I was assigned to a computer science class, and everything changed. Without going into detail, I learned how to manipulate lines on a screen and make them behave like a video game. This initial experience inspired me to dive deeper into the world of programming. For me, programming transformed from curiosity into a true passion.

That being said, I have a very analytical mindset and am constantly asking questions. Even if my program works, I am not satisfied unless I understand why it works, because otherwise, I can't confidently modify or reuse it in different ways in the future. This is why I love programming: it never gets old or boring. Additionally, programming can be applied to virtually any field—from medicine to entertainment—its potential benefits are endless.
